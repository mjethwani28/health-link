Age-wise Basic Health Information

Infants and Toddlers (0-2 years)

  • Ensure proper nutrition for growth and development.
  • Follow a vaccination schedule recommended by healthcare professionals.
  • Practice good hygiene to prevent infections.
  • Provide a safe environment to prevent accidents.
  • Normal heart rate: 100-160 beats per minute
  • Normal blood pressure: 70-100 mmHg (systolic), 50-70 mmHg (diastolic)
  • Normal body temperature: 97.9-100.4°F (36.6-38°C)

Children and Adolescents (3-18 years)

  • Maintain a balanced diet and engage in regular physical activity.
  • Encourage healthy habits like regular handwashing and dental care.
  • Ensure adequate sleep for proper growth and cognitive function.
  • Teach them about the dangers of substance abuse and encourage a drug-free lifestyle.
  • Normal heart rate: 70-100 beats per minute
  • Normal blood pressure: 90-120 mmHg (systolic), 60-80 mmHg (diastolic)
  • Normal body temperature: 97.9-99.5°F (36.6-37.5°C)

Adults (18-64 years)

  • Maintain a healthy weight through a balanced diet and regular exercise.
  • Get regular check-ups and screenings for early detection of diseases.
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques and self-care.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.
  • Normal heart rate: 60-100 beats per minute
  • Normal blood pressure: 90-120 mmHg (systolic), 60-80 mmHg (diastolic)
  • Normal body temperature: 97.5-98.6°F (36.4-37°C)

Elderly (65+ years)

  • Engage in regular physical activity to maintain strength and flexibility.
  • Eat a nutrient-rich diet to support overall health and prevent age-related diseases.
  • Keep the mind active through mental stimulation and social interaction.
  • Take measures to prevent falls and ensure a safe living environment.
  • Normal heart rate: 60-100 beats per minute
  • Normal blood pressure: 90-120 mmHg (systolic), 60-80 mmHg (diastolic)
  • Normal body temperature: 97.5-98.6°F (36.4-37°C)
